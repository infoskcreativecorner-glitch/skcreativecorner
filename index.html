Have a question? Check out the FAQ

Got a question? Explore our FAQ to quickly find answers about SK Creative Corner’s creative services and process.

What services does SK Creative Corner offer?

1. Logo Design & Branding
2. Poster & Flyer Design
3. Website Design & Development
4. Social Media Management
5. Reel Edits & Video Creation
6. Digital Marriage Invitations
7. Motion Graphics & Video Editing
8. Business Card & Brochure Design

Our goal is to make your brand look unique, creative, and professional.

How do I get started with a project?

Contact us through our website or social media. We’ll schedule a short consultation to understand your goals and provide a custom plan with clear pricing and timelines.

What is the usual project turnaround time?

Project timelines depend on the type of work:
Logo / Poster / Invitation: 1–3 days
Reel edits or video projects: 2–4 days
Website design: 5–10 days
Full branding or marketing campaigns: 2–3 weeks

We always ensure quality delivery within your deadline.

Do you offer packages or custom pricing?

Yes! We have both affordable packages for startups and small businesses and custom plans for specialized requirements like video editing or wedding invitations.

Can SK Creative Corner work with clients outside Chennai or India?

Absolutely! We work with clients globally through online meetings, WhatsApp, and Google Drive for file sharing and project delivery.

What if I need revisions?

We provide free revisions (within scope) until you are fully satisfied with the output.
